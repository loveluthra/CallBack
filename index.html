How It Works

Three steps.
Zero effort.

You do this once
01

Add the appointment

Enter client name, phone, service, and time. Takes 30 seconds. Or import hundreds of appointments from a CSV spreadsheet in one click — no reformatting needed.

Fully automated
02

Callback calls them

Our server places a real voice call to the client exactly 24 hours before. No browser open. Works while you sleep, through the weekend, on holidays. The AI handles it.

Guaranteed delivery
03

SMS if no answer

Call not picked up? Instant WhatsApp or SMS fires automatically. The client gets reminded either way. You see every outcome in the live dashboard log.
