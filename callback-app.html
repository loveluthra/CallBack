Connect your Twilio account. 📞
Callback uses Twilio to make calls and send SMS. Each salon needs their own Twilio account — this keeps costs separate and transparent.
💡 Go to twilio.com → Sign up free → Get your Account SID, Auth Token, and a free phone number. Takes 5 minutes.

Callback is ready to start sending reminders. Add your first appointment and watch the automation run.
What happens next:
✅ Add a client appointment
✅ Callback calls them 24hrs before
✅ If no answer, SMS is sent automatically
✅ You get notified only if something changes
